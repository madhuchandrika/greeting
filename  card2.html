A baby rabbit is called a kit, a female is called a doe, and a male is a buck. A group of rabbits is called a herd.

Rabbits are small mammals in the family Leporidae of the order Lagomorpha. Oryctolagus cuniculus includes the European rabbit species and its descendants, the world's 305 breeds of domestic rabbit.Rabbits are herbivores, eating a diet entirely of grasses and other plants.
